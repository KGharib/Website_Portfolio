About Me

Thats when I came accross Data Science, It was such a new world Once I started researching and learning about Data Science I never stopped, it grew into a passion for me and I committed my self to learning everything I could related to Data Science, Machine Learing and everything else I could find online.

From there I began the journey of Self-learning and spent a great deal of time doing every YouTube, Udemy, Udacity course you can think of, and then I made the decesion to Join a Data Science Bootcamp in order to develop my skills to a level where I could see myself making it a full-time career and made some great friends along the way.

My Aim is to be a positive force on people and society through the skils and knowledge I have learnt in Data Science and to make a difference
