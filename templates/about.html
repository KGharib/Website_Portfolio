About Me


After finishing my Degree in Business Management at Brunel University I felt inspired to find something I was more passionate about. I spent some time in self-reflection and did research on the type of career I wanted to see myself in. That's when I came across Data Science, It was a new world and once I started researching and learning about Data Science I never stopped, it grew into a passion for me and I committed my self to learn everything I possibly could related to Data Science, Machine Learning and everything else I could find online.

From there I began the journey of Self-learning and spent a great deal of time doing every YouTube, Udemy, Udacity course you can think of and started applying it in multiple Kaggle projects, and then I made the decision to join a Data Science Bootcamp in order to develop my skills to a level where I could see myself making it a full-time career and made some great friends along the way.

My aim is to meet both a professional and personal goal by being a positive force on people and society and achieving business goals through the skills and knowledge I have learned as a Data Scientist.
